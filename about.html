About Us

Welcome to Radiant institute

At Radiant Institute of Designing, our vision is to bring the best of VFX and animation education to Surat. We understand that many talented individuals aspire to pursue their dreams in Mumbai, but we believe in providing top-notch training right here in Surat at an affordable price.

Our mission is to nurture the aspirations of passionate individuals who are eager to excel in the field of design. We aim to create a supportive environment where students can thrive and realize their potential. Our goal is not just to educate but to inspire and empower our students to become industry leaders.
