Graphic Design

The Graphic Designing in Professional Development course is designed to enhance the skills of experienced graphic designers looking to advance their careers. This course goes beyond the basics and focuses on advanced design techniques, industry trends, and specialized software applications. Students learn about advanced typography, branding, packaging design, and layout design. Additionally, the course covers topics such as design for web and mobile, and interactive design. Emphasis is placed on developing a professional portfolio and honing the skills needed to succeed in a competitive design industry.
